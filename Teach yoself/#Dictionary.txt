Computer Science:

Char Data Type: A variable that stores 1 single character e.g. "a"
Truncation: Shortening a variable e.g. shortening 3.873 to 3
Exponentiation: The repeated multiplication of a variable e.g. 2,4,8
XOR operation: An OR gate following into a NOT gate
Baud rate: The speed data is transmitted in symbols per second
Scope: The width of what we are talking about. Headteacher has a scope of influence over other teachers. Is relative to the thing
Superset: The overall header of a group. Mammal is the superset, deer is the subset
